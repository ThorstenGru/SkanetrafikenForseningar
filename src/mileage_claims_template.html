Not every eligible delay belongs here. This page only lists journeys where the delay itself is beyond reasonable doubt and §4 of the compensation rules ("alternative transport" — own car/mileage) is fully satisfied. compensation.html and claims.html show every network-wide delay illustratively, including unconfirmed predictions and fallback estimates — this page deliberately shows far fewer, on the theory that a Skånetrafiken case handler reviewing one of these should have no real basis to push back. Still not a filed application — verify each one against the Skånetrafiken app before submitting. See COMPENSATION_RULES.md.
