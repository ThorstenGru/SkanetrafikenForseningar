Illustrative estimate only — not a real claim. Figures below are computed from this project's own published rules research (docs/COMPENSATION_RULES.md), which Skånetrafiken can change without notice. Price deduction and alternative-transport (car) compensation are two separate options — the rules do not allow claiming both for the same journey. Applications must be filed within 2 months of the delay, via skanetrafiken.se. Always verify the current rules there before filing.

"Delay used" is the delay at the trip's final stop, or — when that stop was never captured in the realtime feed — the largest delay observed anywhere along the trip instead (marked approx.). Fully cancelled trips are listed for visibility but excluded from the calculation, since the rules don't specify a clear formula for a trip that never ran.
